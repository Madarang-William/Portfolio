Accounting

Course Description

Accounting is the language of business. Our accounting program provides students with a comprehensive understanding of financial reporting, auditing, taxation, and managerial accounting. It equips students with the skills and knowledge necessary to manage financial data, make informed business decisions, and pursue careers in auditing, finance, and accounting firms.
